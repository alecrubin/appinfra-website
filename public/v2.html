The Platform

Industrial Revolution 2.0. Same hills. Same heart. New machines.

Appalachian land.
Appalachian power.
Hyperscale future.

Appalachia Infra assembles large-acreage land positions across West Virginia — reclaimed industrial sites, timbered tracts, brownfields, and greenfield assemblages — and develops them into entitled, powered, fiber-diverse campuses purpose-built for hyperscale data center anchors, driving economic development across the state.

The platform is designed to be a catalyst bringing an increasing flow of AI users to West Virginia — capitalizing on the Appalachian region's vast supply of low-cost power and its welcoming regulatory environment. Structured under the state's microgrid and high-intensity data center framework enabling speed-to-power at scale.

We are the vertically integrated partner for landowners, infrastructure developers, and hyperscalers alike — controlling every layer of the stack from raw acreage to kilowatt delivery.

What We Build

Three verticals.
One integrated platform.

01 —
Land

A resource for WV landowners seeking to capture value from hyperscale interest — curating a portfolio of large-acreage assemblages ideally suited for AI compute campuses. Reclaimed industrial sites, timbered tracts, brownfields, and greenfield positions across the state.

02 —
Power

Behind-the-meter gas generation paired with grid connection, capacity upgrade solutions, and renewable energy integration. Built for speed-to-power at hyperscale — delivering reliable, cost-competitive energy at the megawatt scale AI demands.

03 —
Fiber

Multi-carrier, multi-route diversity to meet hyperscaler requirements — with an in-house design and build team. Four distinct fiber routes delivering the redundancy and throughput that mission-critical compute demands.

The Case for West Virginia

Where AI's hunger
meets America's
energy abundance.

West Virginia offers a rare convergence of structural advantages for hyperscale compute — favorable regulation, abundant power, available land, and a state government actively courting AI infrastructure investment.

Microgrid & High-Intensity DC Framework
WV's purpose-built regulatory framework for high-intensity data centers enables behind-the-meter generation, expedited permitting, and speed-to-power unavailable in other markets.
Low-Cost, Abundant Power
The Appalachian region's energy infrastructure offers some of the most competitive power economics in the Eastern United States — critical for the unit economics of AI compute.
Large-Acreage Land Availability
Reclaimed industrial sites, timbered tracts, and brownfields provide the contiguous acreage hyperscalers require — at economics simply unavailable in coastal markets.
Economic Development Alignment
State and local government are actively engaged in attracting AI infrastructure investment — with tax incentives, expedited processes, and workforce development programs.
