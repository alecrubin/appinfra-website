How we screen sites

  • Acreage under control or option, with documented expansion paths
  • Power and transmission diligence completed before any site is shown
  • Fiber routes and carrier coverage mapped during pre-screening
  • Sized for phased build-out from 50MW first phase to 500MW+ at maturity
  • Permitting and interconnect paths pressure-tested before presentation

Why West Virginia

  • Industrial power rates among the lowest in the Mid-Atlantic
  • Existing high-voltage transmission with available capacity
  • Contiguous parcels above 1,000 acres still assemblable
  • Predictable state and county permitting pathways
  • Within ~400 miles of the Northern Virginia data center cluster
