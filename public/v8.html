What We Do

A single accountable platform.

Appalachia Infra is a West Virginia-based digital infrastructure platform built to serve three constituencies: landowners with underproductive Appalachian holdings, the developers and hyperscalers building the AI economy, and the West Virginia communities that have powered America for a century.

For landowners

A discreet, structured way to unlock real value from underproductive Appalachian holdings — and to participate in what gets built next on the land your family has held for generations.

For developers & hyperscalers

The fastest credible path to a West Virginia campus. We deliver hyperscale-ready sites on a single accountable platform — not a broker, not a land assembler, not an EPC, but one team carrying a project from raw acreage through commissioning.

For West Virginia

Durable economic development — jobs, tax base, and infrastructure rooted in the counties that have powered America for a century. Every campus we deliver is built to stay.

Why West Virginia

The country's next compute corridor.

West Virginia is being rediscovered — not for what it once produced, but for what it can power next.

The Mountain State has emerged as one of the most strategically positioned places in the country for data center development, expansion, and investment. Its energy footprint is unmatched: deep baseload generation, abundant low-cost power, and flexible microgrid capacity that delivers the scale and reliability the AI economy demands.

Geography reinforces the case. West Virginia sits in the middle of the country's primary AI compute corridor — between Ashburn, Columbus, and Atlanta — placing campuses within reach of the markets driving demand.

And the state has made it a priority to clear the path. Permitting, regulation, and policy are aligned to let data centers launch quickly and scale without friction.
