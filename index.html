Mountains to Megawatts

Industrial Revolution 2.0. Same hills. Same heart. New machines. Appalachia Infra is the vertically integrated platform bringing hyperscale compute to West Virginia.

Appalachia Infra assembles large-acreage land positions across West Virginia — reclaimed industrial sites, timbered tracts, brownfields, and greenfield assemblages — and develops them into entitled, powered, fiber-diverse campuses purpose-built for hyperscale data center anchors, driving economic development across the state.

The platform is designed to be a catalyst to bring an increasing flow of AI users to West Virginia to capitalize on the Appalachian region’s vast supply of low-cost power and its welcoming regulatory environment — structured under the state’s microgrid and high-intensity data center framework enabling speed-to-power at scale.
